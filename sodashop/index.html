How to Pay Using GCash QR Code

  1. Open the GCash App
    Unlock your phone. Tap the GCash app. Log in using your MPIN.
  2. Tap “QR” or “Scan”
    On the home screen, look for the “QR” or “Scan QR” button at the top.
  3. Scan the QR Code
    Point your phone camera at the merchant’s QR code. Make sure the QR code fits inside the frame so the app can read it.
  4. Enter the Payment Amount
    If the QR code does not already contain an amount, type the amount you need to pay. Double-check the amount before continuing.
  5. Confirm the Payment
    Tap “Next”. Review the merchant name and payment amount.
  6. Enter Your MPIN
    Enter your 4-digit GCash MPIN to confirm the payment.
  7. Payment Successful
    You will see a confirmation screen. You may also receive a text message or in-app receipt.

✅ Tip: Always check the merchant name before confirming to avoid sending money to the wrong account.
